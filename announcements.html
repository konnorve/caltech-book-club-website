April 2026 Announcement

By Becky Williams

Happy Spring! We have a couple of exciting book club updates, including our new website and upcoming events starting this month.

  1. Check out our new website, thanks to Konnor! It houses all information about book club, including the schedule of upcoming events. Link: https://caltech-book-club.pages.dev/

  2. New monthly books! We can fund 1 book per person. Funding is limited, so please only request a book if you will be able to finish the book in time and attend the discussion. We’ll plan on holding discussions on the last Thursday of the month from 6-7pm in Resnick lobby, with the flexibility to change depending on people’s availability.

    Monthly books (based on poll responses):

    • April: Silent Spring by Rachel Carson (meeting Thursday, May 7th at 6pm)
    • May: The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams
    • June: Surely You’re Joking, Mr. Feynman! by Richard Feynman
    • July: The Silent Patient by Alex Michaelides
    • August: Abundance by Ezra Klein and Derek Thompson
    • September: The Help by Kathryn Stockett

  3. Additional book-related events!

    Weekly Casual Lunches: A place to eat lunch while reading or discussing books with others. Bring your own lunch. Time & Location: Fridays at noon, starting April 10, at the tables between the turtle ponds and Caltech Hall (the tables with the big orange umbrellas).

    Boba & Vroman’s: On Monday, April 13 at 6pm, we’ll meet outside of Resnick and walk to Vroman’s, getting boba along the way.

Please fill out this form by Friday, April 10, to let us know if you’re reading Silent Spring this month, joining for Boba & Vroman’s, and/or want us to buy you a book this term. (Link)
