Info

Meeting location

Meetings are in the Resnick lobby and occasionally the Beckman lawn unless otherwise noted.

Schedule

  • Weekly meetings are often on Tuesdays.
  • Monthly meetings are often on Thursdays.
  • Meetings are typically one hour long.

Reading pace

  • Two main groups: one weekly and one monthly.
  • Weekly group reads about 60 pages each week.
  • A weekly-group book is usually discussed over 4 to 6 meetings.
  • Monthly-group books are usually discussed once per quarter.

Book selection

Books are chosen democratically via polls sent out to the club at the start of each term.

Connect

  • Mailing list: book-club@caltech.edu
  • List host: lists.caltech.edu
  • Questions: kve@caltech.edu

Community

Everyone is welcome, including non-Caltech folks.
